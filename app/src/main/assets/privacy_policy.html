Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 24, 2022

This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures for collecting, using, and disclosing your information when you use this application, as well as your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

As stated in the “Data Privacy Act of 2012” (RA 10173), it is the state's policy to protect the fundamental human right of privacy, of communication while ensuring the free flow of information to promote innovation and growth. Our policies recognize the role of information and communications technology and its obligation to ensure that personal information in our application is secured and protected.

This “Data Privacy Policy” outlines Disaster Online Reporting Application v4's (DORA v4) handling practices for any information that is provided to us when using our application or when any form of data is collected through forms available inside our application.

Our privacy practices

The privacy practices outlined in this Privacy Policy are intended to be in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173) and its rules and regulations. When you use our application and provide us with information in any format and/or medium, including but not limited to forms, you give us permission to collect, handle, store, and share your personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

What we may collect from you

  • Full Name
  • E-mail Address

How we use your information

We use the data collected to understand your needs and provide you with a better service, particularly, for the following reasons:

  • For record-keeping purposes,
  • To provide you with relevant information about recent disasters,
  • To provide you with disasters and evacuation areas near you,
  • And to conduct research and studies necessary to improve our application.

How we protect your personal information

Disaster Online Reporting Application v4 is committed to exercising due diligence to maintain the integrity and confidentiality of all Personal Information provided to us, as well as to protect against unauthorized access, fraudulent misuse, unauthorized destruction, alteration, and all other unauthorized forms of data processing. However, due to the internet's inherent open nature, no data transmission over our applications/services can be guaranteed to be completely safe. We cannot guarantee that the same will not be accessed, disclosed, altered, or destroyed as a result of this and other factors outside our control.

Nonetheless, we will safeguard all personal data gathered in compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012. (RA 10173).

How long we keep your personal information

Your personal information will be kept for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose of this application.
